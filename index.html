About this project

Álvaro Hernández
Finance & Accounting · UC3M Madrid · Class of 2027

FINscope started as a question: can a finance student build a tool that reads real company data and translates it into clear, actionable insight — the way a CFO thinks, not the way a spreadsheet looks?

The result is a fully automated pipeline: live market data, a CFO-calibrated AI model, and sector benchmarking against the 5 closest peers. Built with Make.com, Alpha Vantage, and OpenAI.

Every metric threshold, risk signal and credit decision logic is grounded in real analyst criteria — not just data visualisation.
