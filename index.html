In plain language

🏦
Does the company carry too much debt?
Apple has more cash than debt. Think of it as having your mortgage paid with money to spare. Goldman and ExxonMobil owe significantly more than they generate each year.
💰
Is it actually making money?
Apple and Microsoft keep over 27 cents of every dollar they sell as pure profit. ExxonMobil makes far less per dollar and its profitability swings directly with the oil price.
⚠️
Which one would a bank lend to?
Apple and Microsoft without hesitation. JPMorgan also safe. Goldman warrants monitoring. ExxonMobil is the highest-risk company in this group given debt and energy transition exposure.

About this project

Álvaro Hernández
Finance & Accounting · UC3M Madrid · Class of 2027

FINscope was built to bridge the gap between complex financial data and actionable, human-readable insight. What started as a portfolio project became a fully automated intelligence pipeline.

The system runs autonomously every week: pulling real market data, running it through a CFO-calibrated AI model, and publishing results here — no manual work required after initial setup.

Built with a finance-first mindset: every metric threshold, every risk flag, and every credit decision was designed with real analyst criteria — not just data visualisation.
